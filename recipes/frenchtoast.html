Classic French Toast

Savory and Sweet French Toast

The classic French Toast thats perfect for brunch

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Whisk milk, egg, vanilla, cinnamon, salt together in a big bowl
  2. Butter a pan or a skillet over medium heat
  3. Slice the bread and dunk in the egg mixture, soak both sides
  4. Cook golden for 3 to 4 minutes per side
  5. Serve with chocolate syrup and butter
