Classic and Easy Tomato Soup

Easy 3 ingredient Tomato Soup

The easiest weeknight meal

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Roast the onions and tomatoes in the oven
  2. Transfer them to your blender and blend, then pour the mixture to a pot
  3. Simmer with melted butter for 30 minutes
  4. Serve warm with bread
