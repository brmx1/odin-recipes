Fluffy Delicious Pancakes

Fluffy Old style Pancakes

The classic pancakes to start your day

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk and Butter
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together
  2. Make a well, and then add the wet ingredients
  3. Stir to combine, and scoop the batter onto a hot pat
  4. Cook for two or three minutes per side
  5. When the bubbles form, you flip
  6. Serve with maple syrup
